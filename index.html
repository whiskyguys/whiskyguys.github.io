Welcome to the Whisky Guys Show

Hi, we're the whisky guys Luke & Devin and this is our show about whisky. In each episode we try two different whiskies to see how they compare. For best enjoyment, we recommend drinking along with one or both of the featured whiskies. Or something else. It doesn’t really matter just drink something. If you’d like us to try anything specific, let us know or sponsor a show and we’ll do it.
